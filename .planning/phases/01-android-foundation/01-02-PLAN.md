| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-01-04 | Spoofing | Replayed request (nonce) | mitigate | Interceptor generates a fresh UUID nonce per request (`HmacUtil.generateNonce`); backend enforces Caffeine nonce cache (600s TTL). ASVS L1 V2. |
